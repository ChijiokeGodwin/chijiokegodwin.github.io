What we do

Using photography, we tell stories of the culture, the dreams, the emotions and yearnings of the society. We capture the everyday life, the special moments, the forgotten moments and the memorable ones. We write you a poem with our pictures.
At The Poetographer our pictures are categorized into three. We document the daily hustle, the routine and exceptional moments in our existence. The Poetographer is merely a scribe noting the poetry of nature, rhymes and rhythms that have always appealed to her inhabitants over the ages. The human inhabitants of Earth are just as interesting with their wide range of expressions.

Documentary

  • Quote
  • The hustle & existence

Different paths but same objective, on and on we go, hustling for an existence.

The sights we see each day appears banal but when the sunsets on our existence, we'll pine for those sights. In foreign lands, we'll wish for these familiar lights, sounds and smell.

The Poetographer captures these moments, freezing it through time in a format that can be easy assessed.

Landscape

  • Quote
  • Land & Water

We must tell about the waters and lands of Lagos, its freedom, scars and beauty.

Mother Earth is lovely and should be gazed upon.

The Poetographer captures nature, the aquatic and terrestials, the height and depths, the hidden and discovered, framing it into Different sizes.

People

The beauty and diversity of the faces around us is a story worth telling.

The soul has so many face. We see these faces in the expressions around us, the emotions that define the human existence.
